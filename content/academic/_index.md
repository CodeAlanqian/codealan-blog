## Academic Profile (EN)


### Basic Information

Yiqian Gong (CodeAlan)  
M.Eng. student in Electronic Information, National Academy of Engineering (NAE),  
Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), China.

My current research interests lie in Embodied AI and Vision-Language Navigation (VLN), with a focus on how agents perceive, understand, and act in complex environments under multi-modal guidance.

At this stage I am mainly consolidating foundations in Embodied AI, VLN and RL; this page will be updated with preprints, publications and open-source projects once they become available.

### Research Interests

- Embodied AI and Vision-Language Navigation (VLN)  
- Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Imitation Learning (IL)  
- Large Language Models for Robotics & Navigation  
- Multi-modal Perception and Representation Learning

### Preprints & Publications

- No peer-reviewed publications yet. I am currently focusing on literature review, reproduction of classic works, and building experimental infrastructure for future research.

### Education

- 2024–present · M.Eng. in Electronic Information  
  National Academy of Engineering, SUSTech, China  
- 2019–2023 · B.Eng. in Communication Engineering  
  South China Normal University (SCNU), China  
  · Vice Captain & Vision Group Leader, RoboMaster Team VANGUARD

### Projects & Experience (Selected)

- RoboMaster robot vision and control projects, including target detection, pose estimation, and strategy scheduling.  
- Notes and small experiments around Embodied AI and VLN courses / papers (some being cleaned up into blog posts).  
- Engineering practice on containerized and cloud-based environments (Nextcloud, ROS, LLM services) using Docker.

### Ongoing Directions

- Systematic review of core papers and benchmark datasets in VLN and Embodied AI.  
- Exploring how to integrate large language models into navigation pipelines, e.g., instruction understanding and interpretable planning.  
- Building more engineering-oriented experimental environments (simulation + deployment) for embodied agents.

## 学术简介（中文）

### 基本信息

龚易乾（Yiqian Gong, CodeAlan）  
南方科技大学（SUSTech）国家卓越工程师学院  
电子信息专业硕士研究生（在读）。

目前主要关注具身智能（Embodied AI）与视觉语言导航（Vision-Language Navigation, VLN），侧重研究智能体在多模态信息引导下，在复杂环境中的感知、理解与决策问题。

当前仍处于系统打基础阶段，本页将随着研究进展逐步补充预印本、正式论文与开源项目等信息。

### 研究兴趣

- 具身智能（Embodied AI）、视觉语言导航（VLN）  
- 强化学习（RL）、模仿学习（IL）  
- 大模型在机器人 / 导航中的应用（LLM for Robotics & Navigation）  
- 多模态感知与表示学习

### 预印本与论文

- 暂无正式发表的期刊 / 会议论文，当前主要精力集中在阅读与复现经典工作、搭建实验环境与整理笔记。  

### 教育经历

- 2024–至今：南方科技大学（SUSTech），国家卓越工程师学院，电子信息硕士  
  - 研究方向：具身智能 / 视觉语言导航（VLN）  
- 2019–2023：华南师范大学（SCNU），通信工程本科  
  - RoboMaster 战队 VANGUARD 副队长 / 视觉组长

### 项目与实践（选摘）

- RoboMaster 机器人视觉与控制相关项目：目标检测、姿态估计、策略调度等。  
- 具身智能与 VLN 相关课程、论文的阅读笔记与小规模实验（部分会整理为博客文章）。  
- 围绕 Nextcloud、ROS、LLM 等服务的 Docker 部署与调试实践，侧重工程环境搭建与自动化。

> 更多偏工程实践和配置相关的内容，可以在博客的文章列表中找到，对应标签如 `VLN`、`ROS_NOTE`、`Docker`、`LLM` 等。

### 正在进行的方向

- 系统性梳理 VLN 与具身智能领域的基础论文和经典数据集；  
- 探索如何将大语言模型融入导航策略（指令理解、可解释路径规划等环节）；  
- 打磨更工程化的具身智能实验环境（仿真 + 部署），方便复现、对比与扩展实验。
